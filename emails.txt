Hi Cole and Justin

Thanks for the screenshots and details — and thanks for the clarification on Intuitive Shipping. That really helped clear things up.

Now I have a much better picture of how this should work. Let me lay out the approach so we're aligned:

How the Integration Will Work:

1. A customer places an order on the store
2. Shopify sends a webhook to our server
3. Our system checks the order:
   • Looks at the products — are any tagged as LTL/oversized/freight-eligible?
   • If NO freight products → ignore the order (ShipStation handles it as usual)
   • If YES freight products → check the shipping method the customer selected

4. Based on the shipping method:
   • "Free Freight Shipping" → Fetch rates from FreightClub using Economy LTL carriers
   • "Premier Carrier Delivery" → Fetch rates from FreightClub but only FedEx Freight and Estes

5. Rates are fetched and ready for review

6. You pick a carrier → System books with FreightClub → Tracking is sent to Shopify → Customer gets their shipping notification

Now for step 5 and 6, I have two options for how you'd review and approve the rates:

Option A: Auto-Book (Fastest to Build)
The system automatically selects the lowest-priced carrier matching the tier (Economy LTL for free shipping, FedEx Freight/Estes for Premier) and books immediately. No manual review needed. This is the quickest to build and fully hands-off.

Option B: Notification + Approval Dashboard (Takes More Time)
The system fetches rates but does NOT auto-book. Instead:
   • You receive an email/Slack notification saying "New freight order #1234 — 3 rates available"
   • You go to a simple dashboard page where you see the pending orders with all available rates, carriers, prices, and transit times
   • You pick the one you want and click "Book"
   • System books with FreightClub and handles the rest

This gives you full control (like you mentioned — sometimes you'd pick the better carrier over the cheapest one), but it takes more development time to build the dashboard.


Let me know which option you'd prefer and I'll finalize the estimate and get started.
